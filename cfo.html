Is This For You?

You're ready when...

Not every business needs a full-time CFO. Most scaling businesses need one more than they know.

📊

Your books are clean but you don't know what to do with them

You have P&L but no financial strategy. Numbers on a page don't translate to decisions without interpretation and context.

🚀

You're scaling fast and need financial infrastructure

Systems built for $2M break at $10M. You need someone who's scaled past your current ceiling to help design what comes next.

Board decks, cap table modeling, due diligence prep, and M&A readiness require CFO-level execution — often for a specific window of time.

Data entry and compliance are handled. What you need is someone who can translate the numbers into a plan and hold you to it.
