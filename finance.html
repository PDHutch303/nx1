Curriculum

Three arcs. Twelve weeks.
A complete financial foundation.

Built around a fictional company — Ridgeline Mechanical — that serves as your consistent case study from Week 1 to Week 12.

📊

Arc 1 — Reading the Scoreboard

Income statement, balance sheet, and cash flow statement. You'll learn to read all three and explain them in plain language by the end of Week 4.

🔍

Arc 2 — Making Sense of the Numbers

Financial ratios, benchmarking against industry peers, budget variances, and cost behavior. You'll know what's a problem and what to ignore.

⚡

Arc 3 — Using Numbers to Lead

ROI analysis, red flag detection, talking to finance teams, and a full capstone project. You'll make better decisions with or without a CFO in the room.

Format

Every week follows
the same pattern.

Predictability reduces cognitive load. You build a habit, not just knowledge.

📺

Monday — Content Drop

A 7–9 minute video + 1–2 page reading. Watch on your own schedule. New module unlocks automatically.

💬

Tuesday–Thursday — Discussion

Post your response to the weekly prompt in the cohort community. Minimum 150 words. Reply to at least two peers.

📋

Friday — Worksheet Due

Apply what you learned to Ridgeline Mechanical's financials. Pass/fail grading. Real-world scenarios, not textbook problems.

🤖

AI Tool — 24/7 Access

Claude-powered financial assistant available whenever you need it. Ask questions, test assumptions, explore scenarios.
